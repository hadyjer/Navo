Who We Are

NAVO is a professional driver service built for individuals and businesses that value reliability, safety, and convenience. In a city like Kampala where movement can be unpredictable, we provide a dependable solution — connecting you with drivers you can trust.

Our service is designed to fit into your lifestyle. Whether you need a driver for a few hours, a full day, or on a recurring basis, NAVO gives you flexibility without compromising on quality. Every ride is handled with attention, care, and professionalism.
